Education

A formal education is not necessarily the only way to gain a successful career in the world of IT. A college degree will definitely help but fundamental knowledge can be gained through alterantive sources such as: code camps or coding bootcamps, online training programs like "Udemy", free online tutorials and paid tutorials, just to name a few.

Motivation

In order to truly thrive in anything one must possess the ability to continuously persevere. This path is not a stright line nor is it a cake walk, this rode has many peaks and valleys fraught with a plethora of obstacles. In order to overcome one must bare in mind what they are doing this for, why this is so important to accomplish and if failure is an acceptable outcome.

Revelation

On this journey I have felt devoid of confidence in the skils that I have amassed and at times I have even dealt with "Imposter Syndrome"! After admitiing to myself that this brand of thinking was counter productive to my goals as well as counter intuitive in general I decided to make changes in my approach. Now I am gaining both the skills that I lacked (i.e. hands on experience) as well as the confidence that I never seemed to have regarding my career field.

The path to success is a marathon not a sprint!
